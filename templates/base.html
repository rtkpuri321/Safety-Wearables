About Safety Wearables

Crime against women and children has resulted in both short-term and long-term harm, including psychological trauma and physical injury. Safety wearables, simply put, are any piece of technology that actively alerts the user of a potential or perceived risk in their operational environment and/or provides an actionable solution as close to real-time as possible. Think of a simple wrist band that sends message whenever there is any potential threat.
